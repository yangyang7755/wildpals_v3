# WildPals TestFlight Beta Testing Guide

## What You Need

- An iPhone or iPad (iOS 13.0 or later)
- A free Apple ID (no payment required)
- 5 minutes to set up

---

## Step 1: Create Apple ID (If You Don't Have One)

**Already have an Apple ID?** Skip to Step 2.

1. Go to https://appleid.apple.com/account
2. Click "Create Your Apple ID"
3. Fill in:
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Security questions
4. Verify your email
5. Done! No payment info needed.

---

## Step 2: Install TestFlight App

1. Open App Store on your iPhone
2. Search for "TestFlight"
3. Download the free TestFlight app (by Apple)
4. Open TestFlight and sign in with your Apple ID

---

## Step 3: Join WildPals Beta

**Option A: Public Link (Easiest)**
1. Click this link on your iPhone: [TestFlight Link]
2. Tap "Accept" to join the beta
3. Tap "Install" to download WildPals
4. Done!

**Option B: Invitation Code**
1. Open TestFlight app
2. Tap "Redeem"
3. Enter code: [CODE]
4. Tap "Install"

---

## Step 4: Start Testing!

1. Open WildPals from your home screen
2. Sign up or login
3. Explore the app
4. Report any bugs or feedback

---

## How to Send Feedback

**In TestFlight:**
1. Open TestFlight app
2. Select WildPals
3. Tap "Send Beta Feedback"
4. Describe the issue or suggestion
5. Optionally attach a screenshot

**Or email us:** feedback@wildpals.app

---

## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to pay for TestFlight?**
A: No, TestFlight is completely free.

**Q: Do I need to add a credit card?**
A: No, Apple ID for TestFlight doesn't require payment info.

**Q: How long can I test the app?**
A: Beta builds last 90 days, but we'll send updates regularly.

**Q: Will my data be saved?**
A: Yes, your account and data persist across beta updates.

**Q: Can I stop testing anytime?**
A: Yes, just delete the app or leave the beta in TestFlight.
